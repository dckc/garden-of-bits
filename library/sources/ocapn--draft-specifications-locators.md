> Abstract: The upstream protocol's locator specification (draft). Two locator types: peer locators (find a peer's session-handler; both Syrup and URI serializations) and sturdyref locators (resume a specific capability across sessions; both serializations). Sturdyrefs are the wire-level basis for durable capabilities.
