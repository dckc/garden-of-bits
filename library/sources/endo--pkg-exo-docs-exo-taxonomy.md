> Abstract: Mark Miller's taxonomy of the Exo-making functions, framed along three axes: make-instance vs define-class vs define-class-kit; heap vs virtual vs durable; and (for durables only) make/define vs prepare. The cleanest map of why each form exists in the Exo design space. Five sections cover the overview, each of the three axes, and where the functions live across packages.
